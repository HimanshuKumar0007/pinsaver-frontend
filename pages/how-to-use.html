How to Use PinSaver

Downloading your favorite videos and images from Pinterest is quick and easy with PinSaver. Just follow these simple steps:

1. Copy the Link

Open the Pinterest app or website and find the video, image, or GIF you want to download. Click on the "Share" button and select "Copy Link".

2. Paste the Link

Go to PinSaver and paste the copied link into the input box on the homepage. You can use the "Paste" button for convenience.

3. Download Video

Click the "Download" button. Wait a moment for the video to be processed, then click "Download Video" to save it to your device.
