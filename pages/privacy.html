Privacy Policy for PinSaver

Last updated: January 2026

At PinSaver ("we", "our", or "us"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what rights you have in relation to your personal data.

By using our website, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our services.

1. Information We Collect

PinSaver does not require users to register or create an account and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information such as name, address, phone number, or payment details.

However, we collect certain information automatically when you visit our website.

1.1 Log Files

Like many websites, PinSaver uses log files. These files collect information including:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Date and time of access
  • Referring and exit pages
  • Number of clicks

This information is not linked to any personally identifiable data and is used solely for analytics, site administration, and improving user experience.

1.2 Cookies and Web Beacons

PinSaver uses cookies to:

  • Store visitor preferences
  • Enable site functionality
  • Analyze user behavior
  • Improve performance and user experience

Google and other third-party advertisers may also use cookies, such as the DoubleClick cookie, to show ads based on users’ visits to this and other websites.

Users may decline cookies through their browser settings.

Learn more about Google advertising cookies here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

1.3 Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you contact us by email, we may collect:

  • Your email address
  • The content of your message
  • Any other information you choose to provide

This information is used only to respond to your inquiries and provide support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Improve user experience
  • Understand how visitors use the website
  • Detect and prevent abuse or fraud
  • Respond to user inquiries
  • Serve relevant advertisements

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information to third parties.

3. Google AdSense and Advertising Partners

PinSaver uses Google AdSense to display advertisements.

Google is a third-party vendor that uses cookies to serve ads based on users’ visits to this and other websites. Google's use of the DoubleClick cookie enables it and its partners to serve ads based on users' interests.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting: https://adssettings.google.com

Third-party ad servers or networks use technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or web beacons in their respective ads and links that appear on PinSaver.

PinSaver has no control over these cookies used by third-party advertisers.

4. GDPR Data Protection Rights

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you have the following rights:

  • The right to access your data
  • The right to rectification
  • The right to erasure
  • The right to restrict processing
  • The right to object to processing
  • The right to data portability

To exercise these rights, contact us.

5. CCPA Privacy Rights (California Residents)

Under the CCPA, California consumers have the right to:

  • Request disclosure of data collected
  • Request deletion of personal data
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal data

PinSaver does not sell personal data.

6. Pinterest Content Responsibility

PinSaver is a tool that allows users to access publicly available Pinterest content for personal use.

PinSaver does not host any copyrighted material.

All content is retrieved directly from Pinterest’s public servers.

Users are responsible for ensuring their use complies with copyright laws and Pinterest’s Terms of Service.

7. Data Retention

We retain non-personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Personal data submitted through contact forms is retained only for support purposes.

8. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no online transmission is completely secure.

9. Children’s Information

PinSaver does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe your child has provided information, please contact us and we will remove it.

10. Consent

By using PinSaver, you consent to this Privacy Policy.

11. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page.
